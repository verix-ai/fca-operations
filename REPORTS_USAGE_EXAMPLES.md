## üîç Specific Business Questions You Can Answer

### Question 1: "Are we growing or shrinking?"
**Where to Look**: Total Clients card (top left)
- ‚ÜóÔ∏è +15.3% (30d) = **Growing 15% month-over-month**
- ‚ÜòÔ∏è -5.2% (30d) = **Shrinking, need action**

**Actions Based on Data**:
- **If Growing**: Maintain current strategies, consider scaling
- **If Shrinking**: Review referral sources, check marketer activity, assess market conditions

---

### Question 2: "How fast are we getting clients through the system?"
**Where to Look**: 
- KPI Hero ‚Üí "Avg Throughput" metric
- Throughput Chart (detailed view)

**Interpretation**:
- **25 days average** = Excellent! Under 30 days
- **45 days average** = Slow, investigate delays
- **60+ days** = Critical bottleneck, needs immediate attention

**Actions Based on Data**:
- **Fast (<30 days)**: Document your process, train others
- **Moderate (30-45)**: Look for quick wins to optimize
- **Slow (>45)**: Deep dive into each phase, identify delays

---

### Question 3: "Who are my star performers?"
**Where to Look**: Marketer Performance Table

**Metrics to Check**:
1. **Total Clients** - Overall performance
2. **Last 30 Days** - Recent activity
3. **Avg Days** - Efficiency (lower is better)
4. **Complete %** - Success rate (higher is better)

**Example Analysis**:
```
John Smith:  45 total | 12 last 30d | 25 days avg | 69% complete
Mary Johnson: 38 total |  10 last 30d | 28 days avg | 66% complete
Bob Williams: 32 total |  8 last 30d | 32 days avg | 72% complete
```

**Insights**:
- **John** = Highest volume + fast throughput (25 days)
- **Mary** = Good volume, slightly slower
- **Bob** = Lower volume BUT highest completion rate (72%)

**Actions**:
- **Recognize John** for volume and speed
- **Learn from Bob** about his high completion technique
- **Coach Mary** to improve speed (maybe shadow John?)

**Example Data**:

- Onboarding: 32 days average ‚ö†Ô∏è

### Question 5: "What's our conversion rate from referrals?"
**Where to Look**: KPI Hero ‚Üí "Conversion Rate" metric

**Interpretation**:
- **75%+ conversion** = Excellent referral quality
- **50-74% conversion** = Good, room to improve
- **<50% conversion** = Poor referral quality or intake process issues

**Actions**:
- **High Rate (>75%)**: Your referral sources are great, get more from them
- **Medium (50-75%)**: Qualify referrals better upfront
- **Low (<50%)**: Review referral sources, improve intake process, train marketers

---

### Question 6: "Which marketers need help?"
**Where to Look**: Marketer Performance Table

**Red Flags to Watch**:
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Low "Last 30 Days"** = Inactive or struggling recently
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **High "Avg Days"** = Slow throughput, needs process training
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Low "Complete %"** = Many clients stuck, needs coaching

**Example Problem Case**:
```
Mike Brown: 17 total | 2 last 30d | 48 days avg | 58% complete
```

**This tells you**:
- Very low recent activity (only 2 last month)
- Slow throughput (48 days vs team average of 30)
- Lower completion rate (58% vs team average of 70%)

**Coaching Actions**:
1. **One-on-one meeting**: Understand challenges
2. **Shadow top performer**: Learn faster processes
3. **Set improvement goal**: Get to 30-day average
4. **Check-in weekly**: Track progress

- **Throughput >60 days** ‚Üí Process is broken, needs overhaul

- **Conversion rate 40-60%** ‚Üí Improve intake process

### üü¢ HEALTHY

- **Throughput <30 days** ‚Üí Excellent efficiency
- **Conversion rate >70%** ‚Üí High-quality referrals
