## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

**"All my charts say 'No data'"**
- You need at least 5-10 clients for meaningful insights
- Keep entering data, charts will populate

**"My growth shows +100%"**
- This is normal when starting from 0 or very low numbers
- Percentages stabilize as you add more data

**"I don't see Marketer Table"**
- This is hidden for marketers (they only see their own stats)
- Visible to admins/managers only

**"Throughput seems too high"**
- Remember: This measures from creation date to now
- Older clients will show higher numbers
- Focus on recent months for accurate picture
